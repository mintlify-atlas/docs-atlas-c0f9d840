## What NEXUS does

NEXUS combines a React frontend with a FastAPI backend, Qdrant vector store, and a local Ollama-compatible LLM to give you a complete research workflow:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Paper ingestion" icon="file-pdf" href="/features/paper-ingestion">
    Upload PDFs and TXT files. NEXUS chunks, embeds, and indexes them automatically in the background.
  </Card>
  <Card title="RAG chat" icon="message-bot" href="/features/rag-chat">
    Ask questions about your papers. Every answer cites exact sources with page numbers and confidence scores.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Research insights" icon="chart-network" href="/features/insights">
    Generate structured reports covering consensus findings, research gaps, contradictions, and temporal trends.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Paper writing" icon="pen-nib" href="/features/paper-writing">
    Draft full paper sections — abstract through references — grounded in your library and export as DOCX or PDF.
  </Card>

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the stack">
    Run `docker compose up --build` to start the backend, frontend, and Qdrant vector store together.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload papers">
    Open `http://localhost:5173`, click **⊕ Ingest**, and drop your PDF or TXT files. NEXUS indexes them in the background.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a session">
    Click **⊕ New Session** in the sidebar, give it a name, and NEXUS will automatically include all indexed papers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask, synthesize, write">
    Use the **Chat** tab to ask questions with citations, the **Insights** tab to synthesize across papers, and the **Write** tab to draft your paper sections.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  NEXUS requires a running Ollama instance (or compatible OpenAI-style endpoint) and Qdrant. See the [deployment guide](/deployment) and [LLM setup](/configuration/llm-setup) for details.
</Note>
